Granville Relief Project
What is The Granville Relief Project?

In 2021, Granville Homes wants to shine a light on some amazing local nonprofit organizations who could really use your support. For the month of March, the Granville Relief Project is matching dollar-for-dollar up to $15,000 in contributions made to Live Again Fresno. These critical funds will help Live Again Fresno with their mission to improve the educational attainment and health outcomes for children living on Parkway Drive, an area of Fresno known for drugs and prostitution.

This campaign, and the matching contributions, are being provided, thanks to a partnership between Granville Homes, Mayor Jerry Dyer and Mrs. Diane Dyer, Councilmember Mike Karbassi, Councilmember Miguel Arias, the Fresno Metro Black Chamber of Commerce, and interim Fresno State President Dr. Saúl Jiménez-Sandoval.

As of March 19, we have raised $11,410 towards our goal of $15,000. If you would like to make a donation to help us reach our goal, please visit the Granville Relief Project.
